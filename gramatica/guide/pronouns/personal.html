In Tupi Antigo, the does not distinguish between singular and plural; context will dictate which is more likely.

TIP

The pronouns can equally mean he/she/they/it.

WARNING

In the English language, it is not uncommon to employ the pronouns they/them/theirs in both plural and singular contexts.

As it is important to get used to the Tupian mindset, this text will most frequently translate the as they where it may be more natural to use he/she.
